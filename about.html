About

CET Energy is a multinational energy conglomerate with operations in the upstream, downstream and midstream of the energy spectrum, with focus on Africa. . Through cutting-edge operational delivery across the oil and gas value chain, we are committed to innovative service in the face of rising energy challenges in Africa and across the world.
